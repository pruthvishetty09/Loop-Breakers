Ramada Plaza

Featuring an outdoor pool overlooking the Arabian Sea, Ramada Palm Grove offers 5-star luxury on Juhu Beach. Providing free parking and buffet breakfast, it has a fitness centre and hot tub. Boasting views from oversized windows, rooms at Palm Grove Ramada have a flat-screen TV, safe and seating area. Private bathrooms come with separate shower and bath areas.

Navi Mumbai Marriott

Set in Navi Mumbai, 20 km from Phoenix Market City Mall, Navi Mumbai Marriott offers accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness centre and a bar. With free WiFi, this 5-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. The accommodation provides a concierge service, luggage storage space and currency exchange for guests.

Sun-n-Sand

Located on Juhu Beach, Sun-n-Sand Mumbai offers beachfront accommodation with an outdoor pool, a fitness centre and 5 dining options. Each room at the Sun-n-Sand Mumbai is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, an electronic safe and tea/coffee maker. Cookies, bottled water and a fruit basket are provided.

Novotel

Situated 5.1 km from Chhatrapati Shivaji Airport and overlooking the Arabian Sea Novotel Mumbai Juhu Beach is a beach front property. The hotel features an outdoor pool overlooking the ocean, free parking and 6 dining options. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a cable/satellite TV, minibar and personal safe. En suite bathrooms come with shower facilities.

Citizen Hotel

Directly on popular Juhu Beach, Citizen Hotel Mumbai provides beautiful views of the Arabian Sea, as well as mesmerising sunrise and sunset views. Free parking is provided. Enjoying views of the sea, all air-conditioned rooms come equipped with a TV, sofa and tea/coffee making facilities. En suite bathrooms have a shower. Room service is available 24 hours. Citizen Hotel is a 20-minute drive from Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport. Santacruz Railway Station and Vile Parle Railway Station are within a 10-minute drive away.

JW Marriott

Located along Juhu Beach, JW Marriott Mumbai Juhu has 358 rooms, 3 outdoor pools, the award-winning Quan Spa and 6 dining options including a bakery. Guestrooms are luxurious and offer breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea. The refurbished rooms at the 5-star JW Marriott Mumbai Juhu feature fine beddings and furnishings with a pillow menu. They have both cable and satellite TV, while en suite bathrooms come with large mirrors and a bathtub.
