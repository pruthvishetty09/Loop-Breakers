Top Beaches in Maharastra

Juhu Beach

Smacked right near the city center, Juhu Beach is synonymous to Mumbai. The vast expanse of water stretching across the Arabian Sea glistening under the crimson sun and crashing in a starry night is what makes up for one of the most surreal beaches in Maharashtra.

Ganeshgule Beach

One of the most tranquil beaches in Maharashtra, Ganeshgule Beach offers a breathtaking view of a sunset. While the streaks of orange paint the sky with hue so fine, you can take a romantic walk along the shore and bask in the glory of nature. Stretched along the Konkan Coast, Ganeshgule Beach will lure all you shutterbugs and adventurers to camp under the moonlight.

Let's go
Ganpatipule Beach

Brace yourself to witness the combined timeless beauty of one of the Maharashtra beaches and pilgrimage village only a little far from Ratnagiri in Maharashtra. Being one of the two white sand beaches in Maharashtra, Ganpatipule Beach is a safe haven for photographers and pilgrims who visit Ganpatipule to offer prayers to Lord Ganpati.

Let's go
Revdanda Beach

What if history amalgamates with nature? Get yourself to feel like a character in a Bollywood movie at Revdanda Beach. One of the most fascinating beaches in Maharashtra, Revdanda Beach is one of a kind. The Portuguese fort in its ruined state is surrounded by a stretch of a sea.

Let's go
Mandwa Beach

The pristine beach of Mandwa among the endless beaches in Maharashtra is so much more than a weekend retreat. The water sports along with a view so spectacular that it might take your breath away welcome you with a warm embrace at the Mandwa Beach.
